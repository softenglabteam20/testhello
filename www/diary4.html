New Orleans, November 22th 1951


The nightmares are getting worse and worse. I can't explain his death. It's all so weird. Those things are what's keeping me awake so many nights.

The good part from all this is the only lead I have, this little notebook. All those people referred here were associated with the case and now they are missing or there gone for good. Having read it thoroughly and more than once, I see that Marshal was convinced to investigate the ghetto of our city.

I am heading to the dark corners of Orleans, were oppressed people, drags and superstition are blend in all together.

x

The clock is ticking. Hurry up!
